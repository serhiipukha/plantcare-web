Plant Care features

Green tools for green friends

Smart Watering Reminders

Plant Care uses over 50 data factors, plus local climate and weather information, to determine the optimal time to water your plants and sends you a notification.

... and reminders for fertilizing and more

Plant Cares smart algorithms will also send you notifications when it's time to fertilize, mist, repot, or clean your plants, ensuring they receive optimal care.

Dr Plant Care: Cure sick plants

Plants not feeling well? Yellow leaves, mysterious bugs, weak growth? Dr. Plant Care will diagnose your plant and set up a treatment plan to save your loved one.

Plant Identification

If you aren’t sure what plant you have, just take a picture of it and Plant Care will instantly let you know what it is and what it needs.

Join a plant community

Engage in discussions with fellow plant enthusiasts, share plant care experiences, seek advice, and celebrate successes with a supportive community of green thumbs!

Care Share

Share your plant care schedules with friends and family to keep your green buddies thriving. It’s easy to coordinate watering, fertilizing, and other tasks so everyone stays on the same page.

Plant organization & journal

Keep your garden under control. Plant Care is already helping millions of users collectively care for more than 30 million plants.

Light Meter

Would you like to put a plant in a bathroom without windows? Some plants prefer shade and some are sun-lovers. Get to know which plants are suitable in your home based on the different light conditions of your rooms.
